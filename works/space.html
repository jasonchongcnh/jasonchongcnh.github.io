Origami Simulation

Adjustable Parameters

  • S1: Length of the yellow outer surface component.
  • S2: Length of the red inner surface component.
  • r: Effective tube radius and bending separation.
  • L: Virtual-link length. Adjusting it changes S1 and S2 while preserving their difference.

3D Model

  • The grey structure is a hollow corrugated organ tube.
  • Yellow S1 and red S2 are attached directly to its outer surface.
  • Their paths follow the tube through the bend.
  • Changing any slider rebuilds the tube geometry immediately.
  • Flanges represent the tube’s endpoints.
  • The grid provides spatial scale and orientation.

Transmission Simulation

We also built another simulator, which demonstrates a motorized tape measure controlling a collapsible organ tube.

  • Motor ON: Extends the tape and expands the tube in a straight line.
  • Motor OFF: Holds the current tube length.
  • Retract: Reverses the reel and gradually compresses the tube.
  • Transmission ON: Sends packets through the expanded tube.
  • Packet movement: Packets reflect between the inner tube walls, representing total internal reflection.
  • Controls: Extension speed, maximum travel, load, voltage, packet rate, and packet size.
  • Telemetry: Tube length, expansion, motor power, current, torque, temperature, throughput, latency, reflection count, and delivered packets.
  • Safety behavior: Motion stops automatically at the fully expanded or collapsed limits. Data transfer is blocked while the tube is fully collapsed.
